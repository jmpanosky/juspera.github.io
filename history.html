The second Hunt for History

The second Hunt for History was a pay event taking place in 2003. Teams raced to complete puzzles for points and gain access to special items with loresongs. Many of the puzzles were ingenious, compelling, or downright touching. The Hunt for History items can all be found on the wiki, so I'll limit my treatment to the puzzles themselves. I was lucky enough to be on a team that included Aramana, Armaxis, Charna, Setzier, and Siwas, among others; it goes without saying that there are some laughs in the following logs.

 I wish I had logged every puzzle, but unfortunately this is all I've got. I don't know whether Simu will run these again at some point. If they were to, obviously these logs would be spoilers.
