The log of Juspera's trial, here broken in to six parts, has become one of my favorites. It's like the Griffin Sword Saga in miniature: in it you can see how everyone on both sides of the courtroom, as well as the audience, was bound in a web of relationships -- friendship, love, enmity, betrayal -- that had been tangling together since the beginning of the war. Of course, no trial would be worth witnessing without a few surprises...
