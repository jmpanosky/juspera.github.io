Sorchia, one of the funniest people ever to play the game, sent me this submission on April 17, 2000. It has been sitting in a folder for over nineteen years, waiting for your eyes.


"okay, yah if its not too much trouble to make a section for me, that'd be cool.  just call it sorchia's guide to elanthia or whatever whenever you make it, its basically just gonna be satiric guides like my sorcerer guide.  this doesn't really fit into any category, but I wrote an expose on the mentors if you want to use it...i'm gonna insert it and attatch because i don't know which will work for you."
