GS3 Page - Guides - History - Humor - Roleplaying - The Turnip


The Turnip was created in 2002 as a satire of GS3, Elanthia, Simutronics, the player base, GMs, and anything else that deserved to be made fun of. An equal-opportunity offender, the Turnip was unashamedly based on the Onion, one of our greatest national treasures.
